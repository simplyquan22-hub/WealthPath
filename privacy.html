WealthPath Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect and use your information to provide our services and educational content.

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect essential information to operate and improve our services, categorized as follows:

  • Personal Data You Provide: This includes your name and email address when you contact us directly via email (info@mywealthpath.org) or submit a review/feedback through our designated Google Forms link.
  • Usage Data (Automatically Collected): When you access our website (the landing page), we automatically collect standard information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, pages viewed, and the time/date of your visit. This is primarily gathered through standard web hosting and analytics tools.
  • Third-Party Data: We may receive non-identifiable usage data from linked third-party services like Google Docs (for course PDF access) and Google Forms (for reviews). We do not control their privacy policies; please review them directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected serves the following specific business purposes:

  • To **communicate** with you effectively, responding to your inquiries, questions, and requests sent via email.
  • To **improve service delivery** by analyzing usage data to enhance the design, content, and performance of our landing page and course materials.
  • To **evaluate and enhance** the educational content based on feedback submitted through the review form.
  • To **ensure security** and integrity of our website and services, including preventing fraudulent or malicious activity.

3. Data Storage and Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your Personal Data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Any information you provide via third-party links (Google Docs/Forms) is subject to their respective security measures and policies.

4. Disclosure of Information

We are committed to the principle that **we do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Data to third parties.** We may share generic aggregated demographic information (which is anonymized and not linked to any personal identification) with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the internal purposes outlined in Section 2.

5. Links to External Sites (Third-Party Services)

Our website contains links to third-party services, primarily Google Docs (for the course PDF) and Google Forms (for reviews). Please be aware that these external sites are not operated by us. We strongly advise you to review the separate Privacy Policy of these third-party websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any external sites or services.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the **"Effective Date"** at the top. We encourage you to review this page occasionally for the latest information on our privacy practices.
